Game Rules

  • Bat vs. Ball: Bat wins against Ball.
  • Ball vs. Stump: Ball wins against Stump.
  • Stump vs. Bat: Stump wins against Bat.
  • If both the user and the computer select the same option, it's a tie.
  • The objective is to win more rounds than the computer.
